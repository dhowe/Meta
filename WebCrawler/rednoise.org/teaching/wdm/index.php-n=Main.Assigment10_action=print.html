Assignment #10 (Due 5/8)

2nd Round Final Project Presentations

-- Come to class with projects to show -- they should be close to finished, ready to formally present, with specific questions on which to solicit feedback. For any incomplete or non-working elements, you should have a clear plan on what you want to do and how you want to do it.

-- Final Response Paper

Write a short (1-3 page) response to your experience (in this class) of writing for digital/programmable media. Focus here on ways this might (or might not) affect your future writing/reading practice... Feel free to discuss whatever topics were of personal interest and what, if any, future digital literary projects you might imagine. This should be delivered with the complete documentation of your project. (due 5-17)
