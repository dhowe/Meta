Assigment #5 (Due 3/13)

Reading

-- Read chapter 6 of Charles Hartman's 'Virtual Muse' and be prepared to discuss previous readings from the book (intro, ch1-3 & 5-6).

Writing

-- Write a short (1-3 pages max) response to the Hartman reading (above); focus on things you found particularly interesting/inspirational/insightful and/or things you disagreed with. Send these to me in advance of class, (by monday, 3/12). We may, time-permitting, read these aloud in class.

Programming

-- Midterm project (due 3/20): Choose your favorite piece from the three we've done so far (recombinations, grammars, e-fest projects) and develop it into a finished (short) work.

--- Consider the use of color, space, image, animation and sound (samples, text-to-speech, or you reading) as means to extend the range of the piece in an organic/cohesive fashion. Be prepared to present your ideas and or work-in-progress next week.

--- Although this is due in two weeks, it is highly encouraged that you start right away and take full advantage of lab time for technical issues...
