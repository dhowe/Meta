From New River


In pairs, analyze one of the following works...

  • Start from its technical properties (what platform/language? how does it work? what does the code look like?),
  • Then moved to content (the writing, the images/sounds/video/interactions/etc.) Then your judgment... what do you like/not-like about it and why? Be specific!

Then present these to the class in a VERY brief presentation (~ 5m minutes.) Your options:


  • Alan Bigelow's Cody In Love
  • Andy Campbell and Lynda Williams: Changed
  • Ten Mobile Texts by Nick Montfort
  • Answers Fumbling... by Megan Moriarty
  • Huff and Cabell's American Psycho
  • Sara Bailey's Factography
