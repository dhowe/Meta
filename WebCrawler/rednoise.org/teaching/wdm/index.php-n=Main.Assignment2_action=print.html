Assigment #2 (Due 2/13)

Reading

-- Read Paragraphs on Conceptual Art by Sol Lewitt.

-- Read pages 1-15 of Charles Hartman's 'Virtual Muse' (available at bookstore and library).

Writing

-- Write 4-5 'Recombinant Haikus' as discussed, such that any random selection of lines (Ex. line 1 from Haiku 1, followed by line 2 from Haiku 4, then line 3 from Haiku 3) will 'work'.

Programming

-- Download and run Haiku examples #1-3. See CodeExamples.

-- Replace the place holder text (in the 'Text' tab) in Haiku ex. #3 with your own Recombinant Haikus (above) and change the sketch in some (simple) way to make it your own (color/font/motion/etc).
