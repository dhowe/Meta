On the Free Software used in this class...


As you become more skilled at coding, you may come to notice how non-free (free as in 'freedom', not as in 'beer') software can restrict you. It limits the control you have over your own computer, and it restricts you from sharing work and fixing problems as a community.

As Richard Stallman said, 'Freedom means having control over your own life. If you use a program to carry out activities in your life, your freedom depends on your having control over the program. You deserve to have control over the programs you use, and all the more so when you use them for something important in your life.'

We have chosen to use free software in this class because we value our freedom and would rather not give it up. And because we don't want to be responsible for leading you to surrender your own freedom...

Please read This Page for more about this issue.
