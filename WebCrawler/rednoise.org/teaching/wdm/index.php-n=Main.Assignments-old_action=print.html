New River Journal: Call for submissions (Spring 2014, due May 1)

The New River: A Journal of Digital Writing & Art, a publication of the Virginia Tech MFA in Creative Writing Program, is looking for submissions of new media (hypertext, image/video art, digital text, etc.) for its upcoming Spring 2014 issue. Founded in 1996, The New River is the oldest new media literary journal on the Internet.

The New River seeks to publish work that is interactive, innovative, and fulfills the potential of the digital screen. For the Spring issue, guest editor Josette Torres, a VT MFA alumna and manager of Virginia Tech¹s Center for Digital Discourse and Culture, specifically encourages submissions that address political topics or themes.

Submissions will be accepted via email until May 1, 2014. If accepted, you will be asked to upload all files to our server so we can host it locally. For submission guidelines, please visit the journal¹s website at http://www.thenewriver.us or contact the guest editor via email at thenewriver@vt.edu.
