   Assignment #9    (Due Mon. April 13)

  • By now you should have decided on a final project idea to pursue. For next week, continue to develop your project concept by further refining your idea to the point where you can present it to the class. Each project *must* have a web-page of its own with a description (1 paragraph min.) and at least one piece of explanatory media. These will likely be mock-ups of the interface, which can be useful both for your process and in conveying your ideas to others. Mock-ups can be hand-drawn sketches, images (Sketch-up, Photoshop, etc.) or short procedural demos (Processing, Flash, etc.) If you want to get a head start, prepare a programmatic sketch of one element of your project. Add a link to your project webpage from here.
If you're having trouble deciding, send me a note with the 2 ideas your choosing from.
  • Watch this video by Kenny Goldsmith on so-called 'Conceptual Writing.'
  • Read this short article on 'Regular Expressions in Java'. Then do the coding assignment here. This should be turned in (via email to myself and the TAs) by 4pm on April 13.
